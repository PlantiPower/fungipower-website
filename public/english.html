The Problem

Nutrients are there. Not all of them reach the mycelium.

In an ideal growing cycle, the mycelium absorbs all available nutrients in the substrate. In reality, a significant part remains unused.

This leads to:

  • Uneven colonisation
  • Slower flushes
  • Inconsistent yields
  • Unnecessary nutrient waste
The Solution

FungiPower improves nutrient availability.

FungiPower is a nutrient transporter. It helps the mycelium access more of what is already in the substrate — leading to better uptake, less waste, and stronger performance.

No changes to your current cultivation process needed.
