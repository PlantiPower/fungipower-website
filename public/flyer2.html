The problem

The nutrients are in the substrate, but the mycelium can't access them all. The result: uneven colonisation, slow flushes and inconsistent fruiting bodies, leading to waste and lower yield per cycle.

The solution

FungiPower makes all available nutrients in the substrate accessible to the mycelium. More uptake, less waste, higher yield.
